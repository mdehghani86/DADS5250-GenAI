M00 Production Script DADS 5250 -- Generative AI in Practice

Video 1: "It Changed Everything"

Opening -- 4-5 minutes -- 6 segments
SEG 01 Opening Hook
On Camera ~45 sec Medium close-up

When ChatGPT launched on November 30, 2022, it reached one million users in five days. Instagram took two and a half months. Netflix took three and a half years. No consumer product in history had grown that fast. Something fundamental had shifted.

And I saw it in my own inbox. After generative AI took off, the emails I received started looking... the same. Same structure. Same tone. Same polished formatting. People were using AI to write outreach emails. And honestly -- the emails were fine. Professional. Convincing.

But then I started getting full-page emails from students -- for simple things. Missing a class? One full page. Asking about a deadline? One full page. Things that could be explained in two lines. AI made it easy to write more, but not necessarily better.

And it struck me -- is this really the best use of this technology? Writing longer emails? What if instead of using AI to produce more text, we taught students to use AI to analyze supply chains, build intelligent systems, automate engineering workflows? That gap between what people are doing with AI and what they could be doing -- that is the reason this course exists.

SEG 02 What Is Generative AI
Voiceover + Animation ~75 sec

So what exactly is Generative AI? Think of it this way. Imagine you had a colleague who had read every textbook, every research paper, every technical manual in your field. And whenever you had a question, instead of searching through documents, you could simply ask them -- and they would generate a thoughtful, original answer based on everything they had learned.

That is what a large language model does. It does not search a database. It does not copy and paste. It generates new text, code, or analysis based on patterns it learned from an enormous amount of human knowledge.

The breakthrough came from a 2017 Google paper called Attention Is All You Need by Vaswani and colleagues. That paper introduced the Transformer architecture -- the engine behind GPT, Claude, Gemini, and every major AI system you use today.

SEG 05 Why It Matters for Your Career
On Camera ~45 sec

In every industry, I see two types of professionals emerging. Those who use AI as a consumer -- typing into ChatGPT and accepting whatever comes out. And those who build with AI -- connecting to APIs, designing prompts, building pipelines, deploying agents.

The first group gets incremental productivity gains. The second group builds products, gets promoted, and starts companies.

Jensen Huang, the CEO of NVIDIA, said it best: "You are not going to lose your job to AI. You are going to lose your job to someone who uses AI."

PwC found that AI is linked to a four-times increase in productivity growth -- but only for those who know how to use these tools effectively. This course puts you firmly in the second group.

Video 3: "Course Overview"

Course structure and tools -- 3-4 minutes -- 3 segments
SEG 01 Course Structure
Voiceover + Animation ~90 sec

This course has fourteen modules organized in four phases, plus a midterm hackathon.

Phase One is Foundations. You start by connecting to AI models through code -- not through a chat window. Then you learn to communicate with them precisely. Then you learn to get structured, reliable output back.

Phase Two is Core. You will chain operations together, ground AI in real documents, and build your first systems that can reason and act on their own.

At the midpoint, you put it all together in a hackathon -- a 48-hour sprint where your team builds something real.

Phase Three is Advanced. You will orchestrate teams of specialized AI systems. You will build user interfaces so other people can actually use what you create. You will connect AI to external tools and learn how to keep it safe.

Phase Four is Production. The latest development tools from Anthropic and Google. Workflow automation. And your final project -- a working application for your portfolio.

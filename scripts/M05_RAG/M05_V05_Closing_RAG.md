PART 1: RECAP
------------------------

Think about what you built this week.

You took raw documents, split them into chunks, converted them into embeddings, stored them in a vector database, and built a system that retrieves relevant context and generates grounded answers.

That is not a toy. That is the same architecture behind every enterprise AI assistant you have seen — legal research tools, customer support bots, internal knowledge bases. You just built the core of it.

And here is the key insight. RAG does not replace the language model. It makes the model trustworthy. You give it evidence. It gives you answers you can cite.

That is what production AI looks like.


PART 2: WHAT'S NEXT
------------------------

Next module, we take a big step.

Everything you have built so far follows a script. You define the steps. The model executes them in order. Predictable. Reliable. But rigid.

What if the model could decide what to do next? What if it could look at a problem, pick a tool, use it, evaluate the result, and decide its next move — on its own?

That is an agent. And that is where this course gets really exciting.

See you in Module 6.

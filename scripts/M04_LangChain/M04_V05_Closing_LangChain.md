PART 2: WHAT'S NEXT
------------------------

Next module, we tackle one of the biggest problems in AI — hallucination.

Language models are confident. They sound authoritative. And sometimes, they are completely wrong. They make things up.

RAG — Retrieval-Augmented Generation — fixes this. Instead of letting the model guess, you give it your documents. Your data. Your ground truth. The model retrieves what it needs, then generates an answer grounded in real information.

It is the most important pattern in enterprise AI. And you are going to build one from scratch.

See you in Module 5.

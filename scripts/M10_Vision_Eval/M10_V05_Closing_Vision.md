PART 1: RECAP
------------------------

Your AI can see now.

You sent images to vision APIs and got structured analysis back. You built applications that process visual input alongside text. And you built an evaluation framework -- not just trusting the model's output, but measuring it against ground truth.

That second part matters more than most people realize. Anyone can build a demo that looks impressive. The engineer who can prove it actually works is the one who ships to production.

You have built agents that read, reason, see, connect, and collaborate. You have evaluated their performance. But so far, you have been building everything from code.

In Module 11, we go to the other end of the spectrum. Workflow automation. No-code and low-code tools like n8n that let you wire AI into business processes without writing a single line of Python. And we compare the major GenAI platforms side by side.

You are about to see how the rest of the world deploys AI.
